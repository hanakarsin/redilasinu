Lucy Wanjau: Kenyan Pastor Who Got Married at 43 Says She Had Given Up on Men

March 17, 2024
Post

- CITAM pastor Lucy Wanjau got married at the age of 43

- According to her, she once got into a relationship that did not end well that made her think she will never get married

- She later found her man after visiting a fellow brethren's house who introduced the two of them

Lucy Wanjau, a pastor at Christ Is The Answer Ministries (CITAM), has inspired people after revealing that she got married at the age of 43 and for the first time.

The woman of God was on Rawder Kidula Kedaha's YouTube show 50 Years Of Marriage, where she got candid about waiting for many years to get a partner.

"I waited serving the Lord and I think this made the difference. I have a son who I got when I was 20 years old and who is now 26 years old. When I bore him, I did not like the relationship I was in by then and I concluded that l will never get married or have anything to do with a man,'' she said.

She said she was not born again at the time, and she was desperate to get love, but when things ended, she decided to get born again.

Finding Christ led to so much joy that for a long period, she did not think of getting married and was not in relationships.

She was so much in love with God that she wondered if she could give the man attention because all her focus was on God.

Wanjau revealed that she started doing missions in the Northern part of Kenya, and when she came back to Nairobi, she went to a prayer mountain to seek God to know if she should be married.

But that was the beginning of the self-inflicted pressure. Then, in the last month of December 2017, she decided not to think of marriage or pressure herself about it.

So, in 2018, she went to visit a member who they used to do missions together, he asked her how far she was with her marital goal, and she brushed him off, but he called a friend of his and told him there was a sister-friend.

She left home that evening, and when she got to her house, she received a call from the man who told her that he wants to marry her.

Later, the guy disclosed that the same day they met, he had come from a prayer mountain asking God to help him meet his wife.

The two wedded within seven months.
