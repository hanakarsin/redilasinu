Savannah LaBrant - Age, Family, Bio

Post

Fashion blogger and social media phenomenon best known for running the family YouTube channel The LaBrant Fam, formerly known as Cole&Sav. Her Instagram following exceeds 6 million. The LaBrant Fam YouTube channel features a mix of vlog-like life updates and recaps on different family outings. 

Before Fame

She started dancing at the age of two. She turned her talents as professional photographer into her success as a blogger and Instagrammer. 

Trivia

She and Michelle Foley run an Instagram account called ForEverAndForAva which features their respective daughters, Everleigh Soutas and Ava Foley. She has over 29 million fans on TikTok under her account savv.labrant. 

Family Life

She was born Savannah Soutas. She has a sister named Chantelle and her mother is Deborah Soutas. She married Cole LaBrant in 2017. She and Cole have three children together named Posie Rayne, Zealand and Sunday. Her daughter Everleigh is from a previous relationship with Tommy Smith. 

Associated With

Her sister Chantelle Paige was a member of the R&B girl group Flipsyde.
