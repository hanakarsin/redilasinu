On Thursday, July 14, Tanzanian singer Diamond Platnumz became the topic of discussion after unveiling a brand new Rolls Royce he purchased.

The singer shared videos of a shipping container as it was unloaded outside his Mbezi mansion before the luxury vehicle was driven out.

The Rolls Royce Cullinan purchased by Diamond has a base price of KSh 35 million, without factoring in the shipping fee and import duty.

Only two other African artistes, Davido and Shatta Wale, also own the luxury SUV.

Hence, it came as no surprise that musicians from all over the continent congratulated Diamond after flaunting the Rolls Royce online.

While congratulating him, Zari implied that they had discussed the colour of the vehicle Diamond should buy.

“Glad you chose the colours we agreed on. Congratulations,” Zari wrote.
