Bushiri Back Behind Pulpit after Burial of His Daughter Israella

- During the service, his wife Mary was heard thanking their supporters and also her husband for helping her to accept the will of God

Self-proclaimed Prophet Shepherd Bushiri and his wife Mary Bushiri have resumed their church duties.

The couple took a short break from the ministry while mourning the loss of their 8-year old daughter Israella who died two weeks ago following a short battle with a lung infection.

Heading online, Bushiri announced his return while also taking some time to thank his followers for all their support during their tough times.

During the service, his wife Mary also thanked their supporters for helping her accept the will of God.

"My wife and I are delighted to inform you all that we are back from a short break we took from the pulpit following the passing of our daughter, Saint Israella. As you would recall, we took some days off to be with the Lord as the Lord was dealing with our faith and faithfulness. We also took the opportunity of this break to be with family. "During the break, we received beautiful words of comfort and encouragement from every corner of the globe. We do, immensely, value those words as they, indeed, helped us appreciate the depth of the love you have for us," wrote Bushiri.

The couple's followers, who are always near, quickly headed to the comment section to praise the couple and once again shower them with thoughtful words of kindness.
