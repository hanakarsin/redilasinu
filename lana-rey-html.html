Lana Del Rey - Age, Family, Bio

April 28, 2024
Post

Singer-songwriter whose breakout song "Video Games" propelled her to fame in 2011. Her debut major label album, Born to Die, peaked at number two on the U.S. Billboard 200. 

Before Fame

She received her first recording contract at the age of twenty while performing at a local club. She worked in community service jobs when her first album got shelved by the record company. 

Trivia

Her song "Young and Beautiful" became the theme song for The Great Gatsby in 2013. In 2014, she debuted her song "West Coast" at the Coachella Music Festival. 

Family Life

Her real name is Elizabeth Woolridge Grant. She was born to Rob and Pat Grant and raised alongside her siblings Caroline and Charlie. She briefly dating rapper G-Eazy before splitting in September 2017. She was also briefly romantically linked to Axl Rose in 2012. In 2020, she announced her engagement to musician Clayton Johnson, but they broke up in 2021. In March 2023, she got engaged to music manager Evan Winiker, but they later split. 

Associated With

In 2019, she collaborated with Ariana Grande and Miley Cyrus on the song "Don't Call Me Angel," which is featured in the soundtrack of the Charlie's Angels 2019 remake.
