This Is the Future: Retrenched Workers Open Their Own Successful Farm

May 05, 2024
Post

- An inspirational post about retrenched farm workers who started their own successful farm has been shared online

- The post has explained that the chicken farm workers sell a good number of chickens a day and have also managed to permanently employ over 30 employees

- Netizens have found the post to be life-changing, taking to the comments to express how impressed and inspired they are

A group of retrenched South African farm workers did not allow their fate to become their destiny.

After they were laid off, the group of young men decided to start their own poultry farm in which they reared chicken. They are happy that they never looked back since they began.

The inspirational post that was made by @Mfundoyakhe_S, explains that they now sell over 500 chickens daily and have also managed to permanently employ at least 36 people in their business.

"Former workers of Rainbow Chickens who were retrenched in 2017 have started their chicken farm in Cato Ridge outside Durban 1km away from Rainbow Chickens farms. They sell +500 chickens per day & they have 34 permanent workers. This is how the future of SA economy looks like."

Their efforts won the attention of their local leaders who promised to support them in the business.

"The Provincial government is planning to assist the farm to expand its operations. Siyabonga Ndunankulu...we need more businesses to address the unemployment pandemic." they said.
