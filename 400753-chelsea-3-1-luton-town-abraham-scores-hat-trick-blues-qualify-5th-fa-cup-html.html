Chelsea 3-1 Luton Town: Abraham scores hat-trick as Blues qualify for 5th round of FA Cup

April 02, 2024

- Chelsea vs Luton Town saw the Blues return to winning ways with a 3-1 win over the Championship side

- A Tammy Abraham hat-trick made the difference at Stamford Bridge, with Jordan Clark's first half strike turning out a mere consolation for the visitors

- Timo Werner had a chance to make it 4-1 in the closing stages of the tie after Chelsea won a penalty only for the German to squander the chance

Chelsea are through to the fifth round of the FA Cup round thanks to their 3-1 comprehensive win over minnows Luton Town on Sunday, January 24.

The Blues were desperate to use the tie to turn around their form following a string of poor performances.

And the side made no mistake from the blow of the whistle as Tammy Abraham broke the deadlock after just 11 minutes of game play.

The Englishman found the back of the net after Timo Werner cut in to allow the youngster to seize on the chance.

Abraham doubled his side's advantage six minutes later after nice move from Chelsea that saw the forward nod over Sluga who was off the line.

However, the visitors drew one back on the half hour mark through Jordan Clark who beat Kepa Arrizabalaga with a low shot.

The second half of the match saw the west London side push to put the game to bed but were frustrated with Luton's solid defence.

But with just 16 minutes to end the match, the hosts put the game beyond the Championship side, with Abraham completing his hat-trick of the day after beautiful game play involving Callum Hudson-Odoi and Billy Gilmore.
