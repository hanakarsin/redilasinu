I Gave My 1st Wife Mrs Ngatia 2 Plots but She Still Disturbs Me, Omosh Kizangila

April 26, 2024
Post
  • Former Tahidi High actor Omosh has accused his first wife Mrs Ngatia of portraying him negatively to the public
  • According to him, he had given her two plots in Ndenderu and Thika but she still disturbs him
  • Mrs Ngatia, however, refuted the claims, adding that Omosh was lying to cleanse his name after the backlash on social media

Former Tahidi High actor Joseph Kinuthia aka Omosh has accused his first wife and fellow actress Judy Wacuka aka Mrs Ngatia of being a difficult woman.

According to him, Mrs Ngatia went against an agreement they had and approached the media to accuse him.

Mrs Ngatia has two plots

Speaking exclusively to TUKO.co.ke's Hillary Lisimba, Omosh said that communication between them grounded to a halt since the mother of his children no longer picks his calls.

"We only talk if it's her who calls, but when I reach out the calls go unanswered," he said.

In his words, the couple reached an agreement when he was on holiday in Mombasa that the new house would be occupied by the second wife.

He added that Mrs Ngatia was allocated two plots in Ndenderu and Thika but she refused to settle on them.

Ngatia refutes allegations

When TUKO.co.ke reached out to the actress, she expressed disappointment in her estranged husband for lying to the public to clear his name.

According to her, Omosh is unhappy that she came out with the truth and will do anything to pour cold water on the truth.

"Those plots he's talking about are his father's and he's yet to be allocated. Ask yourself why he didn't let the well-wishers build his house on one of them," she posed.

Omosh, however, remained adamant that everything has been okay between them and only took a downward turn the day he was gifted a new house.

Omosh neglected first family

The back and forth allegations between the one-time lovebirds comes from a recent exposé by Mrs Ngatia that Omosh neglected her and their kids after marrying a second wife.

“He had time for me and my children but since the second wife came, he is always busy. I accepted her as my co-wife but he forgot about us when they moved in together," she told Hiram Maina alias Kamuhunjia.

She accused the thespian of turning cold towards her for no reason and pleaded with the public to help her build a house.
