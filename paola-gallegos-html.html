Paola Gallegos - Age, Family, Bio

June 06, 2024
Post

TikTok star and content creator better known as Calligraphilic, who is a self-described lettering artist. His Calligraphilic TikTok account features calligraphy tutorial videos and knowledge. Her TikTok account calligraphilic has accumulated over 9 million followers. 

Before Fame

She graduated from the University Andina del Cusco with a degree in Tourism. However, she did not realize until many years later when she started her first job as a graduate, at the airport her love of lettering. In her free time during work she used to draw letters in a notebook and imitate the fonts she used to see on the signs of the airport businesses.

Trivia

Her company Calligraphilic was awarded the "Best Creative Entrepreneurship 2019" at the Women Power Peru awards. She also won Best National Vlogger for Cusco Web Fest 2023.
