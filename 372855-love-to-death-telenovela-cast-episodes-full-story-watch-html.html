What is the novela Amar A Muerte about? The show is a mystical thriller that follows the life of a rich media personality, Leon Carvajal. The mogul is a widower with three kids.

He decides to restore his life after meeting and falling in love with Lucia, a beautiful young woman. Unfortunately, on his wedding day, Leon is killed. He ends up being reincarnated in the body of Macario Valdez, a hired killer from Texas.

The assassin, Valdez, dies on the electric chair after killing a judge. His soul is reincarnated in the body of an anthropology professor who dies from a car accident. The two men have to become habituated to life as different people. This becomes the beginning of a very intricate life for them and the people around them.
