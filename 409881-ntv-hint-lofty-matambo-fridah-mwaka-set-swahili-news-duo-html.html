The two revered Swahili news anchors, Fridah Mwaka and Lofty Matambo departed the Standard Group media house without any hints regarding their next destination.

The media personality also thanked the Standard Group for the opportunity they gave him and his colleagues for being by his side.

As for Fridah, she took part in her last broadcast at the station on Sunday, March 21, where she also bid farewell to her fans.
