2015: Buhari Declares For Presidency In Abuja

March 18, 2024
Post

A former military head of state, General Muhammadu Buhari (rtd.), popularly called #GMB on Twitter, is about to formally declare his intention to run for the seat in Aso Rock in February 2015. 

Thousands of All Progressives Congress (APC) supporters have been gathering at Eagles Square, FCT Abuja, since morning of October 15, 2014, Wednesday. The organisers of the rally and the observers emphasised that the event had no crowd buying and no presidential funding.

Follow live comments from the venue below:

14:20 The event is being closed by Senator Abu Ibrahim and Dino Melaye.

The senator emphasised that Nigeria needs someone like Buhari to the rescue. Melaye concluded the rally be expressing hope to gather in May 2015 for the inauguration of Buhari as the new president of Nigeria!

14:03 The rest of the world looks at Nigeria as a home of corruption. Where stealing is not corruption, says Buhari.

13:56 The speech has started. A really powerful quotes by him:

"I humbly wish to present you, before all Nigerians and before God seeking to be elected as APC presidential candidate."

"Nigeria has never been so polarized by a sinking government We in APC have resolved to stop them in their track."

13:50 Prof. Tam David West accompanied #GMB to the podium. The general is about to speak.

13:45 Chief Audu Ogbeh is reading Buhari's complete profile. This looks like the start of the main part of the day!

13:40 Governor Amaechi challenged the ruling Peoples Democratic Party (PDP) to produce the like of GMB or even his half in their party.

13:30 The broadcast of the event by Africa Independent Television (AITOnline) is disrupted. People accuse the network of censoring the rally.

13:17 Hajiya Rabi Ishaq made a speech on behalf of the women.

13:14 The Governor of Rivers State Rotimi Amaechi has arrived the venue.  Governor Babatunde Raji Fashola also came in for Buhari. The Governor of Bayelsa State Timipre Sylva is at the square for more than 1 hour already.

12:37 Gen. Buhari finally enters the venue:

12:12 The Governor of Nasarawa State Tanko Almakura is here to support Buhari.

12:10 Ex House of Reps Member Dino Melaye is giving a speech. Part of it was in Hausa. The memorable quotes from the speech are: "We have decided to rescue Nigeria from the Poverty Development Party," and "PDP has done a lot of damage to our country, this time around we will deal with them."

12:04 Ex Governor of Lagos state and a chieftain of the APC, Bola Tinubu, accompanied by Senator Babafemi Ojudu, is at the sence of the event also!

It would be noted that Buhari took part in 2003, 2007 and 2011 presidential elections. Another veteran politician representing the APC, ex VP Atiku Abubakar, declared his intention to compete on September 24, 2014. Two days ago he picked up the presidential nomination form.
