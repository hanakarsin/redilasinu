Nollywood Actor Turn Amputee Seeks Financial Help

February 09, 2024
Post

Veteran Nollywood actor, Duro Michael has cried out for financial help to replace his amputated leg with an artificial one.

The script interpreter who battled diabetes for a long time, which led to the amputation of his leg pleads financial support and assistance from the public.

A video recently shared on social media reveals the actor’s leg after it was amputated, Naija News learnt.

He was seen sitted on a plastic chair.

“I dey greet una ooh, my brothers and sisters una don see am na. Na diabetes na im cause am ooo,” Duro said.

The actor further pleaded for financial support so as to raise some money and replace the amputated leg with an artificial leg.

“So any one way know wetin him go do, make e help me so I go fix artificial, then I go dey happy. God bless you as you do it in Jesus name,” He stated.

“I am still that your person, still your guy. I still be the same person way I be, God bless you,” Michael said.

He gave out his phone number for anyone willing to help him.
