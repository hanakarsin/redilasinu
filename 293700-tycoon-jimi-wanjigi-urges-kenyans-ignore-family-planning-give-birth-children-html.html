Tycoon Jimi Wanjigi urges Kenyans to ignore family planning, give birth to many children

June 25, 2024
Post

City tycoon Jimi Wanjigi has left tongues wagging after he suggested Kenyans should make more babies in order to achieve certain development.

Citing China and India, some of the world's populous nations, Wanjigi claimed there is greatness in numbers and children in terms of a county's ability to create market for its own produce.

Urging Kenyans to ignore family planning, Wanjigi noted there was no need to worry about the number of children one has since Kenya is teeming with untapped resources enough to feed her people.

"We have enough resources, greater numbers than we are. Greatness is in numbers. People get married, make babies. We want many children in Kenya. China is now a great nation because of numbers, India is over 1 billion, it is a great nation. Closer here, Ethiopia is about 100 million, they are doing great," he said.

For those unable to reproduce in family set ups, Wanjigi asked them to "try elsewhere".

In 2017 in the build up to the General Election, Kiambu Women Representative Gathoni wa Muchomba appealed to men to increase the county population for political advantage.

However, president of Atheists in Kenya Society (AIK) Harrison Mumia on Wednesday, August 22, claimed the country was full and thus no need for more children to be born.
