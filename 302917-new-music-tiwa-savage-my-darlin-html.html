NEW MUSIC: Tiwa Savage - My Darlin

February 22, 2024
Post

From this new one from the Tiwa Savage, it looks like we are not moving on from the popular 'Doro' theme any time soon.

Amidst rumors that her marriage is crashing, Mavin Records first lady Tiwa Savage, has shunned the negative rumors, not allowing it to get to her as she dishes out her first solo official single post her debut album release.

 My Darlin was produced by Don Jazzy and Baby Fresh.  Don Jazzy’s ad-lib makes the already decent song even more enjoyable. And the 'doro' infusion makes it sound like a part two of the much loved Dorobucci song.
