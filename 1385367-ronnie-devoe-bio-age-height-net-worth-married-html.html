Career

His career peaked in the early 1980s after he joined New Edition, an R&B/pop group. In 1984, they released an album that featured Cool It Now, a track which gained massive praise and attention. As a result, the entire album was rated 2x Platinum.

They later created another album called All for Love. The Recording Industry Association of America (RIAA) certified it with Platinum status.

In 1986, the band created their fourth studio album, which they called Under the Blue Moon. Luckily, this one acquired a gold status. After two years, they released yet another album called Heart Break. It featured several tracks such as Can You Stand the Rain, You’re Not My Kind of Girl, and If It Isn’t Love.
