Nkechi Blessing Celebrates Ooni of Ife On 7th Coronation Anniversary

April 02, 2024
Post

Nollywood actress, Nkechi Blessing has taken to her social media page to celebrate the Ooni of Ife, Oba Adeyeye Enitan Ogunwusi on his 7th coronation anniversary.

The Abia-born script interpreter who recently caused a stir after she was spotted twerking before Ooni of Ife at a recent event, took to her verified Instagram handle on Wednesday, to share a photo of herself with the Yoruba monarch as she said a word of prayer for him.

Nkechi also appreciated him for all that he has done for her and everyone far and wide.

“Happy 7th year anniversary Kabiesi His imperial majesty Ooni Babatunde Enitan Adeyeye Ogunwusi Ojaja the second @ooniadimulaife. May your reign continue to be fruitful on the ancestral throne. Thank you for all that you do for me and everyone far and wide. May God bless you abundantly.”

Nkechi Blessing Twerks For Ooni of Ife

Meanwhile, Nkechi Blessing Sunday recently caused a stir after twerking a storm in front of the Ooni of Ife, Oba Adeyeye Enitan Ogunwusi.

Naija News reports that this is coming a few weeks after Nkechi visited the palace of the reverend Yoruba monarch to pay homage.

The actress paid another visit to the Ile-Ife palace for the Queen Moremi pageantry competition, where she is a judge in the beauty pageant.

Nkechi subsequently use the opportunity to showcase her dancing skills and the viral has gone viral online.

The video captured the Ooni seated with other guests at the event and Nkechi literally stole the show by dancing in front of the monarch unbothered.
