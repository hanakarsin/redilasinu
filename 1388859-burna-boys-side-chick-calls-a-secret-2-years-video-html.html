Popular Nigerian singer, Burna Boy, was recently ousted on social media by a lady named JoPearl who claimed to have been seeing him for the past two years despite his relationship with British rapper, Stefflon Don.

In videos posted on Instagram, an emotional JoPearl spoke on how she got to know Burna and how their relationship blossomed to become what it was.

According to her, Burna reached out to her first in her DM and she did not respond for a while but eventually did when her friend convinced her to.

She said that they exchanged numbers and he invited her for one of his shows. Jopearl said Burna was so sweet and caring and made her feel good.

The model added that she was literally staying with him most of the time and that he always wanted to be with her. Jo said what she loved about the singer was how openly emotional he was and how he was in touch with his feelings and expressed them to her.

She, however, said things changed around November when he had to go to Nigeria. He had told her he had to travel ahead of upcoming shows in December.

Jopearl said they continued to keep in touch and he always called her until mid-December when she just could not reach him.

The model recounted how the singer always told her sweet things about how he was going to marry her, how she would give birth to twins for him and how he called her his sweet jollof rice.

Things took another turn in January according to Jopearl when she saw him online with Stefflon Don as his girlfriend. The model said that she literally cried for weeks.

She recounted how he always told her about his love for black women and how he boosted her confidence in ways she didn’t think possible. Jo added that he loved when she cut her hair.

Considering all these, the young lady said she saw his relationship with Stefflon as a publicity stunt and that after a while he reached out to her again and they continued things as normal and just never addressed the elephant in the room.

Jopearl said they had been seeing each other for two years and she has been in love with him. She added that he always said he would come back to her and she is his baby.

According to the model, he never mentioned Steff to her face.

In another video, Jopearl said she was tired of protecting the singer and that he could not always eat his cake and have it.

The model said seeing him secretly for two years had affected her mental state and that she had lost friends as a result.

Jo added that she had not been able to give anybody a chance for the past two years and had been stuck up in seeing him.

The model said she wasn’t doing the video to hurt him and that she still loved him but wanted to break the cycle. She added that as far as Burna was concerned they were on good terms and that she knew two years was a long time to bring up all these feelings of resentment but that seeing him hurt her more and was breaking her.
