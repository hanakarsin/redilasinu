Gee Nelly - Age, Family, Bio

May 15, 2024
Post

Social media influencer best known for her geenelly Twitch channel, which has amassed over 170,000 followers. She streams content from games such as Overwatch on her channel. She is also popular on Instagram and sure runs multiple YouTube channels. 

Before Fame

She worked for Ralph Lauren after being scouted in John Lewis. She studied creative media in college.  

Trivia

Her glitter company, GLOW, which she co-runs with her friend Gigi, sold out in under 5 minutes their very first launch. She has also been a big fan of Korean Pop music and has published multiple videos about her K-pop idols on YouTube. 

Family Life

Her father passed away when she was three; she is very close with her mother. She has dated YouTuber TheBurntChip.  

Associated With

She has been roommates with fellow YouTuber WillNE.  She's friends with pop singer Talia Mar.
