Iyabo Ojo's Daughter Gets People Talking As She Goes Extra Mile For Enioluwa's Birthday

March 19, 2024
Post

Nigerian Influencer cum video vixen, Priscilla Ojo, the daughter of Nollywood actress, Iyabo Ojo, has got many people talking over the huge surprise to her best friend, Enioluwa on his birthday.

Naija News reports that Enioluwa celebrated his 24th birthday on July 6, 2023, and Priscilla gave him a memorable birthday surprise.

Iyabo Ojo took to her Instagram page to share the gigantic cake her daughter ordered for Enioluwa on his birthday.

Reposting the cake from the baker’s page, the thespian called on her friends to give her a similar surprise for her next birthday.

The post read: “@olufunmi_aj @shantizworld @divalicious_mbl_mrs_o come and see #bestiegoals Dec 21 is round the corner

“I got a call from @its.priscy to do a surprise birthday cake for her bestie @enioluwaofficial, and we delivered under 24 hours

“Guy, you know @enioluwaofficial love, good food. we did everything he loves, and all craft is edible cake and sugar”
