Its Actually Side Effect Of What Ive Been Battling

May 23, 2024
Post

Former Big Brother Naija housemate, Munirat Antoinette Lecky has opened up about battling a health condition that has led to her weight gain.

This comes after the reality star via her Twitter page on Tuesday, July 11th shared a recent photo of herself looking quite chubbier than her usual self thus stirring curiosity amongst her fans.

Replying those who were curious about her sudden weight gain, the educator cum entertainer appreciated everyone who had reached out to her concerning her new looks.

She further stressed that her weight gain came about as a result of a health condition she’s currently battling, adding that she is self-conscious about it.

Anto, however, did not name what health condition she’s battling but rather assured her fans she’ll hit the gym when she’s better.

She wrote: “Thanks to everyone for the kind words about my weight gain. While I like it a little, I’m still self-conscious because it was not intentional. It actually the side effect of a health condition that I have been battling. But inshallah, I will be healed soon and going to the gym.”
