Tonto Dikehs Son, King Andre Bags Endorsement Deal

July 15, 2024
Post

The son of award-winning Nigerian actress, Tonto Dikeh, King Andre, has bag an ambassadorial deal with a beauty product brand.

The actress announced the development on her Instagram account on Thursday.

The five-year-old was welcomed on board as the new ambassador of the beauty product brand.

Tonto in her post wrote, “Introducing the brand new ambassador of the [name withheld] brand, it’s the smart intelligent and handsome KINGY @kingandre_dikeh.

“With so many carcinogens out there, [name withheld] as a natural skincare brand aligns with my purpose of being sure of what goes onto my son’s skin.

“I am excited to have King onboard [name withheld] on the train.”

In another development, fast-rising Nigerian actress, Ngozi Chiemeke on Tuesday was shot dead, at Ika North Local Government Area of Delta State.

Chiemeke was reportedly shot dead at her Point Of Sale shop on Tuesday, 7th December 2021, on Deeper Life Road, in Boji Boji, Owa, Ika North Local Government Area of Delta State.
