Daniel Ndambuki’s role in Kenyan comedy

Born in 1977, Churchill has grown to be a celebrated Kenyan comedian and radio host working at Classic 105. He was born in Kutui, but his parents moved to Machakos. The father of Kenyan comedy, as he is referred to by many, has been in the industry since 1996 and has grown to be a household name in the country. The comedian launched the comedy show Churchill Live in September 2008. However, in its third season (2012), the show changed its name to Churchill Show.
