Nigerian gospel singer, Mercy Chinwo is currently holding her white wedding with her lover, Pastor Blessed of Water Brooks Church, Lekki Lagos.

Naija News reports that the event which is being held in Port Harcourt, has many Nigerian celebrities in attendance.

Videos and photos of the event which emerged online captured the couple all excited as they celebrate their big day.

A video that got many people talking online is the moment when the master of ceremony burst into tongues as Mercy and her husband kiss.

Mercy Chinwo Announces Engagement

Mercy Chinwo shocked many Nigerians when she announced her engagement in July to Lagos-based clergyman, Pastor Blessed of Waterbrooks.

The singer took to her Instagram page to share the good news with a photoshoot of their moments together and captioned it with a heartwarming message.

She wrote: “I am blessed to be blessed with the blessed official. The thoughts of doing the next phase and the rest phase of my life with you makes the blessing more real… I love you, Sweet”.
