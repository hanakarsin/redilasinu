Lilian Nganga Shows Off Well-Built Trainer in Adorable Photo

March 08, 2024
Post
  • Former Machakos county first lady Lilian Nganga introduced her fitness trainer through a social media post
  • Lilian shared a photo with the well-built man, saying she has not been going to the gym
  • The fitness enthusiast, dating rapper Juliani, said she made it up to her trainer by running with him

Lilian Nganga, the former wife of Machakos governor Alfred Mutua, has shared an adorable photo with her handsome fitness trainer.

The fitness enthusiast shared the selfie on her Instagram stories on Saturday, September 25.

Lilian posed with the well-built trainer wearing a vest in the photo, showing off his bulging muscles.

Lilian wore a red cap and, in the caption, admitted that she had missed gym a couple of times in the recent past.

The philanthropic beauty said she made it up to her trainer by going on a run with him.

“My gym attendance is super wanting so I made it up with a run with the trainer," she wrote.

Finding happiness

The beauty, who is currently dating renowned rapper Juliani, recently offered words of wisdom to her social media followers.

Lilian said a relationship could not bring happiness via a social media post on Wednesday, September 22.

According to the post shared by Lilian, one cannot also derive happiness from a job or money.

"Happiness starts with you. Not with your relationship, not with your job, not with your money, but with you," the post she shared read.

Juliani romance

In a recent interview, Juliani disclosed that he met with Lilian for the first time in June.

The rapper revealed that she liked Lilian immediately after meeting her, thanks to the qualities she possesses.
