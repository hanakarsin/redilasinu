'It Is Time To Pay Tinubu Back'

August 06, 2024
Post

The Governor of Lagos State, Babajide Sanwo-Olu has appealed to lawmakers at the state and local level to show their support towards the presidential aspirations of Bola Ahmed Tinubu.

The governor made the plea during the 17th Edition of the Executive/Legislative Parley, with the theme: ”Mobilizing Citizens as a Social Capital Towards 2023 General Elections, at Ikeja, Lagos on Friday, Nan reports.

He stated that Tinubu had given so much to the state when he was governor and it was time to return the favor and pay him back for his good deeds.

Sanwo-Olu said: ”This, indeed, is the time to pay him back with our unwavering support. He is, no doubt, the right and best man for the job. He knows the issues plaguing our nation like the back of his hands and he has the magic wand to fix it.

“So, because of our Apex leader, Asiwaju Bola Ahmed Tinubu, it is time for us to take Lagos to Nigeria agenda. It is time for us to work assiduously and ensure that all of these successes in Lagos can be reflected in Nigeria as a whole.

”That is why I am calling on all of us to ensure that we do everything we can do politically and otherwise, to ensure that we can help to activate and actualise the next president of Nigeria, Asiwaju Bola Ahmed Tinubu.

”Let us rally behind our great party, the All Progressives Congress, let us rally behind the great man that indeed, we believe can help us take our country to the next level.”
